MEAN REVERSION LONG
* RSI2 bellow 5
* Sum of RSI readings for the past 2 days < 20
* Lower low 3 days in a row
* Lower close 3 days in a row
* Close low than lowest low of 5 past days
* Market close 1% bellow previous day close and ATR(5) > ATR(10) [increased volatility]
* Market close 1% bellow previous day close and ATR(5) < ATR(10) [decreased volatility]
* Close stretches bellow EMA(5) more than 50% ATR
* Close stretches 1% bellow EMA(5)
* EMA(5) decreased 0.5% compared to previous day 


* Bollinger bads pass bellow lower band with the RSI bellow 30 (oversold) -> buy  and sell at middle band
* Bollinger bads pass above upper band with the RSI bellow 70 (oversold) -> buy  and sell at middle band
* stop loss 2 ATR
